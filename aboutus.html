About Us

Global accessories Is a operated company that start in 2005 in Penang, Malaysia. Over the past 5 year, Global accessories has grown to be one of the most trusted wholesale phone accessories in Malaysia. The mission of the company is to provide high quality product to the customers. We are constantly updating our websites to stay up to date on accessories for all the latest phones and tablets. Our website products including casing, power bank, screen protector, earphone and headphones, cables and others. We offer a variety of exclusive brands such as Remax, Pineng, Pisen and others.
